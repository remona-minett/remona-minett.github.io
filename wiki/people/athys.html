Athys

Athys is a main character introduced in the Prologue and revealed by name at the end of the same chapter.

Athys has sapphire-blue eyes, middle-back length black hair, white skin, and is fairly muscular. She is around 6 feet tall.

Generally, when other people try to interact with her, she is reserved, sometimes hostile. When she initiates conversation with someone, her words are chosen and to the point.

She is travelling during most of the story, attempting to meet her final goal of proving her accusations to be true to Ivrasil.
